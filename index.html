About the Author

Dedicated to Making Chemistry Accessible

Sudhir Nama is a dedicated Chemistry educator, author, and the founder of Chemistry Spark Publication. With a mission to democratise quality science education across India, he has crafted a range of books that prioritise deep conceptual understanding over rote memorisation.

His publications are carefully structured to bridge the gap between textbook theory and competitive exam demands. Whether you are preparing for JEE Advanced, NEET, CSIR-NET, or GATE, each title provides a clear, logical pathway through complex chemical concepts — complete with solved examples, reaction mechanisms, and practice problems.

Beyond writing, Sudhir Nama produces free educational video content, making high-quality chemistry instruction accessible to students regardless of their socioeconomic background. His teaching philosophy centres on clarity, logical reasoning, and exam-oriented problem solving.
